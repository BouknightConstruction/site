About Us

Bouknight Construction was founded in 2016 by Jeff Bouknight to provide quality construction solutions to the greater South Carolina area. With 20+ years of experience, our small team ensures attentive and high-quality service on every project.

Our Mission

We strive to deliver high-quality, cost-effective homebuilding and remodeling services through honest communication, expert craftsmanship, and dependable service — building trust and community along the way.

Why Choose Us

We emphasize trust, quality, and commitment. Our locally rooted team listens carefully and communicates clearly — ensuring transparency, accurate estimates, and honest timelines on every job.
